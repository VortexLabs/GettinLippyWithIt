The Charity

Since 1996, Kids in the Hall Bistro Program has operated as a social enterprise, helping at-risk youth between the ages of 16 & 24 to gain knowledge and skills necessary to be successful in an educational or employment setting. Capacity of these young people is built through a combination of support, life skills, training and participation in work experiences at the Bistro from a 4-8 month period. Follow up and support is included where they are provided case plans, job search and educational planning.
